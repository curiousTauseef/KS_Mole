Before Home Depot , Nardelli had been a senior executive at General Electric Co. . The appointment of a CEO with no background in the auto industry appeared to signal an effort to bring fresh blood into the company as it becomes independent . Cerberus has , however , named Wolfgang Bernhard , who had been a senior executive at DaimlerChrysler and Volkswagen AG , as Chrysler 's chairman .
