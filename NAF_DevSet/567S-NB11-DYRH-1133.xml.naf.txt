Purchasing-manager surveys will also be relaesed Wednesday in Europe . The Stoxx Europe 600 index ( xx : sxxp ) rose 0.3 % to 262.05 , led by a 1.2 % rise for HSBC Holdings PLC ( uk : hsba ) ( hbc ) . Standard Chartered PLC ( uk : stan ) rose 2.33 % on the back of earnings . The FTSE 100 index ( uk : ukx ) rose 0.4 % to 5,658.49 . French banks were also boosting the CAC 40 index ( fr : px1 ) , up 0.6 % to 3,311.92 . Shares of Societe Generale SA ( fr : aca ) rose 2.7 % after the French bank posted a profit fall but was upbeat . The German DAX 30 index ( dx : dax ) rose 0.1 % to 6,780.58 , with BMW AG ( de : bmw ) down 2.7 % after posting a profit fall . © 1997-2008 MarketWatch.com , Inc. . All rights reserved .
