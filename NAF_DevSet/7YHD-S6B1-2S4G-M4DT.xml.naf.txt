U.S. markets reversed early losses to finish in the black on the one year anniversary of the collapse of financial giant Lehman Brothers . Japan 's market moved lower on relatively light trading volume on Monday , pushed by fears that a strengthening yen will hurt the exporters . The benchmark Nikkei 225 Average declined 242.27 points or 2.32 percent to close at 10.202.06 . Among the Nikkei component stocks , there were 15 closing higher , 207 were lower and 3 finished unchanged . The broader Topix index was down 16.4 points or 1.7 percent at 934.05 . Among the major exporters , auto maker Honda fell 85 yen or 2.97 percent , rival Toyota Motor was down 100 yen or 2.60 percent , tire maker Bridgestone was down 55 yen or 3.30 percent , Canon was down 120 yen or 3.30 percent and Subaru maker Fuji Heavy was down 21 yen or 5.50 percent . Japan Airlines shares rose 13 yen or 7.98 percent on reports that both American Airlines and Delta Airlines were in talks to acquire a share of JAL . Among the steel makers , Nippon Steel dropped 9 yen or 2.59 percent , JFE Holdings was down 10 yen or 0.31 percent , Sumitomo Metal Industries was down 4 yen or 1.73 percent and Kobe Steel fell 3 yen or 1.79 percent . In the financial sector , Mitsubishi UFJ Financial was down 9 yen or 1.65 percent , Mizuho Financial was down 4 yen or 1.96 percent , Sumitomo Mitsui was down 50 yen or 1.35 percent and Resona Holdings shed 24 yen or 1.92 percent . In overseas trading , U.S. stocks spent much of Monday 's session on the down side , with traders concerned about a budding trade tariff dispute between the U.S. and China . Stocks turned positive in the final hour of trading however , and managed a higher close . At the closing bell on Wall Street , the Dow Jones Industrial Average was up 21.39 points or 0.22 percent at 9.626.80 , the Nasdaq Composite was up 10.88 points or 0.52 percent at 2,091.78 and the Standard & Poor 's 500 index was up 6.61 points or 0.63 percent at 1,049.34 . Nearly all of the major Asia/Pacific region markets closed lower on Monday . Hong Kong 's Hang Seng index dropped 229.22 points or 1.08 percent to 20.932.20 . China 's Shanghai Composite index managed a gain of 36.95 points or 1.24 percent to close at 3.026.74 . In India , the BSE/SENSEX index was down 50.11 points or 0.31 percent at 16,214.19 . Indonesia 's Jakarta Composite index declined 33.24 points or 1.38 percent to 2.382.70 . The KOSPI/Seoul Composite index in South Korea was down 16.79 points or 1.02 percent at 1,634.91 .
